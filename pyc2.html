COLLECTION TWO

THE RUN DOWN

    In the second Pyer Moss Collection titled American, Also. Lesson 2 named Normal Jean-Raymond focused on telling stories of the common black family experience. The Pyer Moss team led the installation with a runway show at the historic Weeksville Heritage Center. Followed by runway shows in Shanghai & Lagos. Then they followed the physical shows with the Director X led film Seven Mothers. A film that focuses on the family life of Kerby Jean-Raymond and the 7 women who raised him after his mother passed away in 1994.
