Your Aesthetic is Y2K 💿

~Y2K~

Y2K is an aesthetic that was prevalent from roughly 1998 to 2003. Named after the Y2K Bug, it is characterized by a distinct aesthetic period, encapsulating fashion, hardware design, music and furnishings shining with tech optimism, sometimes literally. Some of the styles aspects include tight leather pants, silver & dark eyeshadow, shiny clothing, Oakleys, gradients, and Blobitecture.A good brand to shop for this Aesthetic would be Gucci's SS00 collection.
