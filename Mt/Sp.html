~Steampunk~

I like to think of Steampunk as a cross between Victorian and Cyber Goth. The style is the Cyber Goth of the Victorian Era; a futuristic, bronze cog-filled world of futuristic inventions and adventures with flares of mechanical genius and Victorian fashions. A good brand to shop for this Aesthetic would be Diors SS10 Collection.
