Your Aesthetic is Victorian Gothic 💀

~Victorian Gothic~

A common way to dress for Goths is a suit with a top hat for men, cravats and trousers also a common form of dress. Anything that might remind one of an aristocrat in the Victorian era. Same goes for women, who usually adore large ballgowns, corsets, hats, long black gloves, and plenty of jewelry with large, dark colored stones. A good brand to shop for this Aesthetic would be Burleska.
