Your Aesthetic is Dark Academia 🔮

~Dark Academia~

The main inspiration for Dark Academic fashion comes from 1940's prep school uniforms. Elements of the 1950's suburban American preppy look are often incorporated, such as sweater vests or sweaters worn tied over the shoulders. The main colour scheme is black, grey, beige, brown, cream, ivory, dark green, burgundy. Dark and almost gothic elements are often added to emphasize the gothic nature of the Aesthetic. A good brand to shop for this Aesthetic would be Chanel's SS20 Couture Collection.
