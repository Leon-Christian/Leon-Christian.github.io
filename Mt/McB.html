Your Aesthetic is McBling 💎

~McBling~

People often associate this aesthetic with the color pink (usually a bright hot pink) as it was popularized by Paris Hilton during this time. The McBling aesthetic began around 2003 with the start of the Iraq War and premiere of Paris Hilton's The Simple Life. It ended around 2008 with the Great Recession, Barack Obama's election, and the backlash against 2000s culture setting in. A good brand to shop for this Aesthetic would be Christian Cowan.
