BACKGROUND & HISTORY

WHO? WHAT? WHEN?

  • Pyer Moss was founded in 2013 by Kerby Jean-Raymond
  • Jean-Raymond describes the brand as an art project or a timely social experiment at times
  • Pyer Moss aims to use its voice and platform to challenge social narratives and evoke dialogue.
  • Pyer Moss continues to redefine itself every season with collections and runways that combine storytelling, activism, debate, theatre and social commentary
